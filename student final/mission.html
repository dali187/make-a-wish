Our Mission

Why do we do what we do?

Here at Make a Wish there is nothing that puts a bigger smile on our faces than a smile on a childs face. This is why our mission statement is "Together, we create life-changing wishes for children with critical illnesses." because it is how we provide smiles.

We partner with volunteers, donors, and medical professionals to ensure each wish is crafted with care and purpose. Many children say their wish was a turning point in their treatment—and we’re honored to be part of that transformation.
